Italy

Colosseum

Crazy lines of people! Amazing to hear the stories of what this building was used for while standing inside.

Florence

Florence is by far my favorite city in Italy! Lots of good food, lots of beautiful art, lots of good shopping...what more could you ask for?? This is the best view, up on Michealangelo Hill.

Vespas in the Tuscan Countryside

Best activity by far!! We rented scooters in Florence and drove them through the beautiful vinyards and country houses outside the city, got some good pizza, and proceeded the 2+ hour drive back.

Roman Forum

So cool to see the ruins of the Roman people. This comes with the ticket to the Colosseum and it is huge and so fun to walk around.

Sorrento

Right outside of Naples, is Sorrento, near the Amalfi Coast. It is beautiful and would be the perfect place to go in the summer...we just went in March, the water was cold but swimming in that beautiful blue water was worth it.
